Sol Solutions

Sol Solutions was produced in 48 hours by four Notre Dame computer science students. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly experience to assess how solar panel implementation can benefit you. We hope you see the benefits of switching to renewable energy as a sustainable measure to help our Earth.


Sustainable energy does not harm the environment and can help improve public health. Renewable energy resources emit little to no greenhouse gases, which is better for the environment and our health. The smog produced by fossil fuels irritates our lungs and can lead to lung diseases and respiratory conditions.
